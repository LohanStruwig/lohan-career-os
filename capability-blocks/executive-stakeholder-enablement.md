# Executive Stakeholder Enablement

## Capability Statement

Executive stakeholder enablement through decision frameworks, business-case classification, roadmap materials, prioritization templates, strategic translation, and practical leadership communication.

## Evidence

- Created executive-facing roadmap materials and decision frameworks.
- Trained executive stakeholders on AI and adoption scenarios.
- Helped leaders compare commercial, pilot, readiness, capability, and market-readiness initiatives.
- Served as a bridge between regional leadership, country/market leadership, product/publishing, commercial/legal, customer service, and technology partners.

## Reusable Resume Language

Enabled executive decisions by translating digital ambiguity into roadmap choices, business-case categories, prioritization templates, AI awareness, operational tradeoffs, and implementation-ready workstreams.
