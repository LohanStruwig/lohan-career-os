# Core Positioning

Digital Transformation and Product Operations Executive with experience across EdTech platforms, digital publishing, AI enablement, product/platform operations, and B2B digital systems.

## Expanded Positioning

I connect strategic intent to operating reality. My work often sits between executives, product teams, publishing or content teams, technology partners, sales, customer service, finance/legal, and market-facing stakeholders.

The recurring pattern is the same: clarify the business model, map the workflow, define ownership, build governance, create feedback loops, establish QA checkpoints, and help teams move from scattered initiatives toward repeatable operating practice.
