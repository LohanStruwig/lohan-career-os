# Leadership Operating Style

## Practical Strategy

I translate strategy into working systems: roadmaps, ownership models, governance routines, visible workflows, launch readiness, QA checkpoints, and decision frameworks.

## Cross-Functional Translation

Much of my work involves translating between groups that think differently: executive leadership, publishing/content teams, product teams, finance/legal, customer service, sales, marketing, vendors, and technical partners.

## High-Structure, Low-Theater Execution

I prefer visible work, clear tradeoffs, evidence-backed prioritization, and operating models that can survive real delivery pressure.

## Builder-Operator Bias

I am comfortable moving between executive framing and hands-on product execution. VentureLayer Labs and CNC Intel Hub reflect this founder/operator layer: strategy, product design, data workflow, QA, GitHub delivery discipline, and deployment thinking.

## Personal Operating Note

A sports background across golf, rugby, boxing, and kickboxing shapes the working style: disciplined, competitive without being chaotic, analytical under pressure, and comfortable building repeatable practice loops until the system performs.
