# Product Operations Roles

## Best Positioning

Product operations leader who creates the workflows, QA loops, launch readiness, stakeholder handoffs, customer feedback systems, and governance practices that help product teams deliver repeatably.

## Strong Evidence

- platform readiness work at Macmillan/Springer Nature Curriculum
- CNC Intel Hub product ownership
- QA and deployment discipline
- product workflow definition
- cross-functional handoff design

## Keywords

Product operations, platform operations, launch readiness, release governance, QA, customer feedback, stakeholder handoff, product lifecycle, go-to-market readiness.
