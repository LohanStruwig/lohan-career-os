# Chief Of Staff And Strategy Ops Roles

## Best Positioning

## Strong Evidence

- executive-facing roadmap materials
- business-case classification logic
- opportunity prioritization templates
- cross-functional stakeholder coordination
- AI executive enablement
- operating model design

Strategy operations, chief of staff, executive alignment, decision frameworks, operating cadence, roadmap, prioritization, governance, cross-functional leadership.
