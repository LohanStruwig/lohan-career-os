# CNC Intel Hub Future Case Study

## Draft Case Study Shape

### Problem

B2B industrial teams need better ways to review companies, territory context, dealer/customer fit, evidence quality, and planning outputs without losing the logic behind their decisions.

### Approach

Build a focused market-intelligence workspace that turns company research, evidence, filters, saved views, and exportable planning outputs into a repeatable workflow.

### Execution

Use AI-assisted product development with human approval gates, GitHub version control, React/TypeScript frontend delivery, Supabase-backed data workflows, Vercel deployment, QA loops, and data governance.

### Evidence

Approximately 500 commits in one month during an intensive build cycle.
