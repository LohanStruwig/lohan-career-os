# VentureLayer Labs

## Purpose

VentureLayer Labs is the internal product-development and product-studio function of VentureLayer Digital LLC.

## Focus Areas

- productized digital systems
- market-intelligence workflows
- AI-assisted product development
- governed client workspace design
- QA and release discipline
- data governance
- human-in-the-loop product decisions

## Current Anchor Product

CNC Intel Hub serves as the first major anchor product for the lab, combining industrial B2B market context, AI-assisted product development, product workflow design, and technical execution.
