# EdTech Platform Operations

## Executive Snapshot

Lohan's regional digital operations work included EdTech and digital publishing workflows across MEE, LMS/eBook access, MOTC teacher professional development, voucher-based eBook distribution, Shopify/eCommerce models, TVET digital content, content readiness, and platform-readiness initiatives.

The work demonstrates platform operations, launch readiness, digital publishing transformation, customer-support handoff, content workflow coordination, and cross-functional governance.

## Context

Digital education platforms require more than content digitization. They require access models, metadata, entitlement logic, customer support, platform readiness, content preparation, teacher/learner onboarding, reporting readiness, data privacy, and market-specific implementation planning.

## Challenge

The challenge was to translate global platform capability into practical regional business models and operating workflows that could be launched, piloted, repositioned, supported, or sequenced based on readiness and market constraints.

## Role

Lohan led and supported regional digital operations across platform-readiness work, digital publishing workflows, teacher-development platform support, LMS/eBook models, voucher/eCommerce models, TVET digital content, and customer-ready implementation planning.

## Approach

The approach focused on turning platform capability into operational reality:

- map platform access and entitlement workflows
- coordinate content readiness and metadata requirements
- connect ISBN tagging, voucher redemption, and eCommerce handoff
- prepare customer-support and launch-readiness workflows
- support teacher-development platform alignment
- coordinate publishing, sales, marketing, customer service, finance/legal, and technology partners
- build QA and feedback loops for post-launch improvement

## Capability Demonstrated

This case study demonstrates EdTech platform operations, digital publishing operations, product lifecycle support, support-readiness planning, stakeholder handoff, launch governance, and productized workflow design.
