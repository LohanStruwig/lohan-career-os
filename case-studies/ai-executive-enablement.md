# AI Executive Enablement

## Executive Snapshot

Lohan initiated executive AI awareness and enablement work for approximately 14 EXCO/executive stakeholders, covering AI concepts, LLMs, AI agents, disruption scenarios, and practical implications for education publishing and digital workflow design.

The work demonstrates AI enablement, executive communication, responsible adoption framing, human-in-the-loop governance, and the ability to translate frontier digital systems into practical operating questions.

## Context

AI adoption creates strategic and operational questions for leadership teams: what the technology can do, where disruption may appear, how workflows may change, what governance boundaries are needed, and how teams should experiment responsibly.

In education publishing, these questions connect directly to content workflows, digital learning materials, platform operations, customer expectations, and product strategy.

## Challenge

The challenge was to help executive stakeholders understand AI in a way that was practical, commercially relevant, and connected to real operating decisions rather than abstract trend language.

## Role

Lohan initiated and delivered AI awareness and enablement work for approximately 14 EXCO/executive stakeholders, translating AI concepts into implications for education publishing, platform workflows, digital learning, and responsible experimentation.

## Approach

The approach combined executive translation with operating-model thinking:

- explain AI concepts, LLMs, and AI agents in practical language
- connect disruption scenarios to education publishing and digital learning workflows
- explore implications for content, animation, platform, and product workflows
- frame AI as an awareness and experimentation layer with controlled adoption boundaries
- emphasize human approval gates, QA loops, and responsible workflow design

## Capability Demonstrated

This case study demonstrates AI enablement, executive stakeholder alignment, frontier digital systems thinking, responsible AI framing, workflow transformation, and human-in-the-loop governance.
