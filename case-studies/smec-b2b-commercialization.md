# SMEC B2B Commercialization

## Executive Snapshot

At SMEC Projects Africa, Lohan led marketing, digital presence, brand development, exhibitions, supplier/channel support, and sales enablement for a B2B industrial business spanning CNC machine tools, automation, retrofitting, tooling, consumables, and turnkey manufacturing solutions.

The work demonstrates B2B technical-commercial positioning, industrial market fluency, sales enablement, brand infrastructure, and translation between technical capability and customer-facing value.

## At A Glance

- Domain: B2B industrial commercialization
- Role: Chief Marketing Officer
- Capability demonstrated: Technical-commercial positioning, sales enablement, supplier/channel support, brand infrastructure, and industrial market communication
- Public-safe proof type: Role summary, market-facing operating patterns, selected commercialization themes

## Context

B2B industrial markets require credibility, technical clarity, supplier/channel alignment, practical sales enablement, and market-facing communication that can support relationship-led and proposal-driven opportunities.

## Challenge

The challenge was to connect technical product categories, supplier relationships, customer-facing materials, exhibitions, digital presence, and internal sales support into a more coherent commercial operating model.

## Role

Lohan served as Chief Marketing Officer, supporting leadership and sales teams with positioning, product-market communication, supplier/channel messaging, exhibition readiness, and industrial-market credibility.

## Approach

The approach focused on technical-commercial translation:

- build B2B industrial brand and market-positioning infrastructure
- connect digital presence to sales collateral and exhibition readiness
- support supplier-brand coordination
- translate CNC, automation, retrofit, tooling, and manufacturing-solution capability into customer-facing business value
- align technical and non-technical stakeholders around product categories, use cases, and market opportunities

## Capability Demonstrated

This case study demonstrates B2B commercialization, technical-commercial communication, sales enablement, industrial market understanding, channel support, and the CNC/VMC fluency later applied to VentureLayer and CNC Intel Hub.
